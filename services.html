What We Do

Full service, start to finish

Service 01

New Home Design

Building a new home is one of the biggest investments you'll ever make. We make sure you get it right — designing a home that's beautiful, functional, and built around the way your family actually lives.

We work with you from the very first sketch, helping you make smart decisions about layout, orientation, materials and budget. We then prepare all documentation for council consent and work alongside your builder through construction.

Serving Auckland, Whangarei and Northland.

Start your new build

Service 02

Extensions & Renovations

Love your location but need more space? We design extensions and renovations that feel seamless — as if they were always part of the original home.

Whether it's an open-plan living extension, a new master suite, or a full interior transformation, we'll help you unlock your home's potential without the upheaval of moving.

We handle design, consents and builder coordination from start to finish.

Discuss your renovation

Service 03

House Relocations

Relocating an existing house onto a new site can be a highly cost-effective way to get a character home without starting from scratch. We design the foundation, additions and alterations to suit your new site and your vision.

We manage the full design process and council consents, and connect you with reputable house movers and builders who specialise in this type of work.

Available across Auckland, Whangarei and Northland.

Talk to us about relocations

Service 04

Consents & Documentation

Dealing with councils can be time-consuming and confusing. We prepare all your consent documentation — resource consents, building consents, and any supporting reports — and manage the submission process on your behalf.

We know what Auckland Council and Whangarei District Council need, and we write applications that are thorough and clear to minimise delays and RFIs.

Small consents, LBP sign-offs and certificate of title queries also welcome.

Get consent support

How It Works

Our process

01
Free Consultation

We start with a free call or meeting to understand your project, your site, your budget and your timeline. No obligation — just a conversation.

02
Concept Design

We develop initial design concepts and work through them with you until the layout and form feel exactly right. This is the creative heart of the project.

03
Consents

We prepare a full set of drawings and documentation for building consent and handle the council submission and any queries on your behalf.

04
Construction

We work alongside your builder during the build — answering questions, making site visits and making sure the finished result matches the design intent.
