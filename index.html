About the Studio

Passion, creativity,
and a little playfulness

We're a residential architecture studio based in Auckland and Whangarei, working across New Zealand. We believe a home should have soul — designed around how you actually live, not just how it looks in photos.

From your first consultation through to the day the builder hands over the keys, we're with you every step of the way — simplifying the process, connecting you with the right people, and making sure the result exceeds your expectations.

What We Do

Full service, start to finish

01
New Home Design

A home built exactly for you — your site, your lifestyle, your budget. We design new builds that are beautiful to live in and built to last.

02
Extensions & Renovations

Make your existing home work better. We design extensions and renovations that feel like they were always meant to be there.

03
House Relocations

Moving a house onto a new site is a cost-effective way to get more space. We handle the design and consent process from start to finish.

04
Consents & Documentation

Resource consents, building consents, council submissions — we prepare everything and manage the process so you don't have to.
