Who We Are

We design homes
built around your life

4C Architecture Studio is a residential architecture practice based in Auckland and Whangarei, working with clients across New Zealand. We specialise in new homes, extensions, renovations and house relocations.

We believe a home should have personality — it should reflect who you are, work beautifully for how you live, and be designed with care down to the last detail. That's what we set out to achieve on every project.

We're also practical people. We understand budgets, timelines and councils. Our job is to make the whole process as straightforward as possible — guiding you from that first conversation all the way through to the finished build.

What Drives Us

How we approach every project

01
We listen first

Every project starts with understanding you — your lifestyle, your family, how you use your home day to day. Great design comes from listening, not assuming.

02
Detail is everything

We're meticulous about the details — because it's the details that make a home feel considered and crafted, not just built.

03
Designed for living

A beautiful home that doesn't work well isn't a good home. We balance aesthetics with function, so your space looks great and feels even better.

04
We're with you throughout

From your first sketch to handing over the keys, we're your advocate — coordinating builders, consultants and councils so you don't have to.
